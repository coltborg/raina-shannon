I'm a Designer based in Chicago.

My background is in visual design, but a few years ago I made the switch to product + web design. These days, I am most excited by, and wish to grow in, Interaction Design (IxD) across devices.

I enjoy puzzling over complex information and untangling workflows — making it easier to understand and navigate; building accessibility and cohesive frameworks; as well as creating artful digital spaces.

But when I'm not working, I can generally be found checking the day's astrology (do tell me your rising sign), experimenting with different art media, bouldering, digging into a sci-fi or fantasy novel, or biking about the city.

I also host the cutest cat: Pictures upon request.
