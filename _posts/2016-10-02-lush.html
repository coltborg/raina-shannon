LUSH is an international skincare & cosmetics company with a passion for green intiatives & sustainability, product-testing ethics, & safe, nature-based products with a fun, non-traditional edge.

With that in mind, the rebrand objective is two-fold; to appeal to the company's goals of using sustainable packaging & minimizing waste (while still asserting brand identity) as well as to unify the aesthetics of hundreds of diverse products while not competing with the look of the products themselves.
